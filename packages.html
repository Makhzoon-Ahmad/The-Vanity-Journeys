Kashmir Explorer — 3 Star

3 Nights / 4 Days · Srinagar · Sonamarg · Gulmarg

Day1

Arrival in Srinagar

On arrival at Srinagar airport, our representative will meet you and take you for Srinagar local sightseeing covering Dal Lake, Mughal Gardens, Cheshmashahi, and Hazratbal Shrine. After sightseeing, check in to hotel. Dinner and night stay in Srinagar.

Day2

Srinagar → Sonamarg → Srinagar

After breakfast, leave for Sonamarg (84 km) — "The Meadow of Gold" at 2730m altitude. The Sindh River meanders through the valley. Ponies can be hired for a trip to Thajiwas Glacier. Return to Srinagar. Overnight at hotel.

Day3

Srinagar → Gulmarg → Srinagar

After breakfast, proceed to Gulmarg (Meadow of Flowers). Experience one of the best ski slopes in the world. The Gondola Cable Car offers rides to Kongdori (14,000 ft) with views of Himalayan peaks. Evening return to Srinagar. Dinner and overnight at houseboat on Dal Lake.

Day4

Departure

Check out from hotel. Transfer to Srinagar Airport or Railway Station for your onward journey. Farewell with beautiful memories of Kashmir.

Valley Explorer — 3 Star

4 Nights / 5 Days · Srinagar · Sonamarg · Gulmarg · Pahalgam

Day1

Arrival in Srinagar

Arrive at Srinagar International Airport. 35-minute drive through the heart of New Srinagar, passing the Abdullah Bridge on the Jhelum River. Proceed for an hour Shikara ride on Dal Lake. Overnight stay in hotel.

Day2

Srinagar → Sonamarg → Srinagar

Full day excursion to Sonamarg (84 km, 3 hrs) — "Meadow of Gold". A gushing river flowing down from snowy heights with dense woodlands. Adventure options: treks, sledging, angling, alpine skiing and white water rafting. Enjoy pony ride or leisurely walks. Return to Srinagar. Dinner and overnight.

Day3

Srinagar → Gulmarg → Srinagar

Full day at Gulmarg (56 km, 2 hrs) at 2730m altitude. Leisurely walks on lush green meadows with snow-capped mountain backdrop. Enjoy pony ride or gondola ride (at own cost). Return to Srinagar. Dinner and overnight.

Day4

Srinagar → Pahalgam → Srinagar

Drive to Pahalgam. Visit Betaab Valley and Chandanwari. Horse riding trip to Baisaran meadows (at own cost). After sightseeing, drive back to Srinagar. Transfer to houseboat for overnight stay on Dal Lake.

Day5

Departure

After breakfast, check out and transfer to airport with memories of Kashmir.
