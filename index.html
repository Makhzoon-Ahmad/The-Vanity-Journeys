FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of packages do you offer?
We offer adventure tours, cultural experiences, honeymoon getaways, family packages, and fully customized itineraries tailored to your interests and budget.
Can I customize my travel itinerary?
Absolutely! We specialize in creating personalized itineraries to match your interests, travel dates, and budget — ensuring a truly unique experience.
What is included in your tour packages?
Our packages typically include accommodation, transportation, sightseeing, guided tours, and selected meals. Specific inclusions vary by package.
How do I make a booking?
You can book through our website, by calling us, or via WhatsApp. We offer flexible payment options including an initial deposit with the balance payable before travel.
Do you offer hotel bookings separately?
Yes, we offer standalone hotel and houseboat booking services across Kashmir for a comfortable, hassle-free stay.
Is customer support available during the trip?
Absolutely! Our team provides full support throughout your journey to assist with any concerns or emergencies.
